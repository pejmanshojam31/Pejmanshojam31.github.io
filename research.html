Research Overview

My research focuses on predictive modeling, radiomics, and machine learning in the context of biomedical engineering and mathematical biology. I aim to develop innovative solutions for personalized treatment strategies and in silico clinical trials.

Current Projects

  • AI Framework for Tumor Spheroids: Developing predictive models for tumor spheroid dynamics using radiomics data.
  • Glioblastoma Modeling: Refining mathematical models for glioma-macrophage interactions and conducting in silico clinical trials.

Past Projects

  • Drug Distribution in Tumors: Mathematical modeling of drug delivery in solid tumors.
  • Cerebral Aneurysm Modeling: Patient-specific modeling of cerebral aneurysms.
